Client Data History

Client historical data are maintained in a separate record of the "history of client data", You can display the client tab in the Info Panel or via the button "Assist" ("drill to detail" (Shift + F8)) next to the relevant fields. Establishing the historical record the user makes by overriding existing values in a particular field on the client. As soon as the changing field of recoil will save the original record in history.
