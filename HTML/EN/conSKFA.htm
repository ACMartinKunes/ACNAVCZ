Functionality Fixed Assets has been extended in the Slovak localization in the field of tax depreciation.

If we want to ensure the depreciation calculation based on the number of months, it is necessary to place a check mark in Fixed Asset Setup field Deprec. Reduction First Year. When entering the depreciation parameters, it is necessary to correctly fill in the Depreciation Starting Date field. Depreciation starting date must always be on the first day of the month. Standard report Depreciation calculation will be used for calculation of depreciation. In the report for Depreciation calculation do not enter the Force No. of Days field. Calculated depreciation amount is automatically inserted in FA journal. After posting, FA entry is created, that has value in Unclaim Depr.First Year Amount field. The unclaimed proportion of annual depreciation shall be applied in the year following the expiry of the period of depreciation.

Technical upgrade in the Tax Depreciation Book must be posted on 1.1. of corresponding year.
